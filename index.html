Attend-Ace is a free progress tracking platform developed by The Nerd Herd for educational institutions that aims to simplify creating, distributing and grading student reports on the basis of their attendance marked.

Features

Attend-Ace is your one place for keeping track of your attendance and progress report. Our easy-to-use and secure tool helps educators manage, measure, and enrich organizational experiences.

Attendance Record

Student Attendance records are for seeing the regularity of the student attending the class for any educational institutions. It enables the teachers to maintain the record of students present or absent in their class for a whole academic year.
